What services do i offer?

I specialize in custom graphic design and social media management. From logos and branding to engaging social media content, I create designs that help businesses stand out. I also offer social media strategy and profile optimization to boost your online presence.Additionally, I design simple, responsive websites and landing pages to showcase your brand and support your marketing efforts.I’m here to deliver affordable, high-quality services to help you grow your brand and connect with your audience.

Was benötige Ich von Dir, um ein Projekt zu starten?

To get started on a project, I’ll need to know your goals and vision for it. What are you hoping to achieve? Understanding your target audience is also important so I can tailor the design and content to them. I’ll also need any existing materials you have, such as logos or images, along with your design preferences, like colors and style. It’s also helpful to know your budget and timeline for the project, as well as any specific features or requirements you may have. With all this information, I can create a plan that fits your needs and helps you reach your objectives.

How long does a project take?

The duration of a project depends on several factors such as the complexity of the project, the scope of work, and your specific requirements. For smaller projects like designing logos, creating social media graphics, or developing simple branding materials, it can take about 1-2 weeks to complete. Larger and more complex projects, such as developing a full branding strategy or creating a comprehensive social media campaign, may take several weeks to a few months depending on the details and revisions needed. Clear communication, timely feedback, and a well-defined plan can help speed up the process.4o mini

How much does my work cost?

The cost of a project depends on its scope, complexity, and the services required. For smaller projects like logo design or social media graphics, prices typically start at a fixed rate. Larger and more complex projects, such as full branding packages or extensive social media management, will be priced based on the amount of work involved and the time required. I always strive to offer affordable and transparent pricing, and I’ll provide a detailed quote after discussing your specific needs and requirements. If you have a budget in mind, feel free to share it, and we can work together to create a solution that fits your goals and budget.

Do I offer revisions?

Yes, I offer revisions as part of my services. I want to make sure you are completely satisfied with the final result, so I provide a set number of revisions depending on the project. After the initial design or concept is delivered, you’ll have the opportunity to provide feedback, and I will make adjustments as needed. If additional revisions are required beyond the included amount, I can offer them for an additional fee. My goal is to ensure that the final product aligns perfectly with your vision.4o mini
